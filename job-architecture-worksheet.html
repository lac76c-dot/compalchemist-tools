Section 1: Role Inventory

Enter all current job titles. These flow into every section that follows.

✦ Start hereEnter every job title in your organization — one per line. Look for numbered titles (e.g. "Specialist II"), vague descriptors, or legacy names. Flag those in the Legacy Titles field below.
👥

Section 2: Job Families

Group your titles into job families. Select the family name, then assign titles from your inventory.

✦ What is a job family?A grouping of roles that share a common work focus, skill set, and career path. Select a family from the dropdown (or choose Other to type your own), then click Included Roles to assign titles. Aim for 5–10 families.

Section 5: Communication Plan

Governance, manager briefings, and rollout communication — the part most organizations skip.

✦ Don't skip thisManagers must be briefed before employees. Messages should explain what stays the same, what changes, and why it benefits people — not just the org. If you have unionized staff, your collective agreement may require consultation before implementation.

Rollout Checklist

  • Manager training materials prepared
  • Employee communication drafted and approved
  • Leadership approval secured
  • Rollout timeline established
  • Union consultation completed (if applicable)
  • Collective agreement reviewed for conflicts
  • Governance process defined (owner + review cadence)
  • Compensation review linked to level mapping
  • Legal / HR legal review completed
